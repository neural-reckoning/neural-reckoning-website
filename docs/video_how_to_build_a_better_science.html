How to build a better science

Talk given as part of a discussion panel on institutional reform at MRI Together 2022. I discussed institutional problems in science and how they are exacerbated by the for-profit academic publishers. I gave concrete recommendations for what we could do to make things better, and discussed Neuromatch Open Publishing.
