Pengfei Sun

  • Lab member: 2025-
  • Google Scholar
  • ORCID
  • Supervised by: Danyal Akarca, Dan Goodman

Pengfei Sun works on developing brain-inspired neural networks, with a particular focus on delay learning, neuromorphic applications, and EEG decoding.

Pengfei is supervised by Dan Akarca and co-supervised by Dan Goodman.
